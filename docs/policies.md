# 📜 VibMedia — Policies & Legal Documents

> Living legal framework for VibMedia's AI influencer agency operations. These documents cover terms of service, privacy, influencer agreements, and brand service terms.

---

## 1. Terms of Service

### 1.1 About VibMedia

VibMedia ("we", "us", "the Agency") operates an AI-assisted influencer marketing agency connecting food content creators ("Influencers") with restaurant brands ("Brands") in Delhi NCR, India.

### 1.2 Services Provided

- **For Influencers:** Profile enrichment, brand matching, job board access, campaign coordination, content QC, payment processing
- **For Brands:** Influencer discovery, outreach, campaign management, content quality review, performance reporting

### 1.3 Eligibility

- Influencers must be 18+ years old
- Influencers must have an active, public Instagram account with 1,000+ followers
- Brands must be registered food/beverage businesses in Delhi NCR
- Brands must have a valid contact number for communication

### 1.4 Account & Points System

- Influencers earn points for completed campaigns as per the reputation tier table
- **Points expire after 12 months of account inactivity** (no job completion)
- Tiers: Bronze (0-49 pts) → Silver (50-199 pts) → Gold (200-499 pts) → Platinum (500+ pts)
- Tier benefits include priority matching, premium brand access, and performance bonuses

### 1.5 No-Show Policy

| Offense | Consequence |
|---------|-------------|
| 1st no-show | Written warning via DM |
| 2nd no-show | 7-day ban from Job Board |
| 3rd no-show | Permanent tier demotion (e.g., Gold → Silver) |
| 4th+ no-show | Account suspension pending manual review |

> A "no-show" is defined as accepting a job assignment and failing to complete the scheduled visit without 24-hour advance notice.

### 1.6 Commission Structure

- VibMedia charges a **20% commission** on all paid influencer deals
- Commission is deducted before payout to the influencer
- Example: Brand pays ₹10,000 → VibMedia keeps ₹2,000 → Influencer receives ₹8,000
- Barter-only deals are commission-free

### 1.7 Content Standards

- All content must be original and created by the assigned influencer
- Content must adhere to the campaign brief (hashtags, mentions, deliverables)
- Content must not contain offensive, discriminatory, or misleading material
- VibMedia reserves the right to request content revisions via AI QC review
- Final content must be approved before posting

### 1.8 Payment Terms

- Brand payments are due within 7 days of campaign completion
- Influencer payouts are processed within 5 business days of brand payment receipt
- Payouts via UPI transfer (or bank transfer for amounts above ₹50,000)
- Minimum payout threshold: ₹1,000

### 1.9 Termination

- Either party may terminate the relationship with 7 days written notice
- Pending campaigns must be completed before termination
- Earned but unpaid commissions will be settled within 30 days
- VibMedia reserves the right to immediately terminate accounts involved in fraud, fake engagement, or policy violations

## 2. Privacy Policy

### 2.1 Data We Collect

**From Influencers:**
| Data Type | Purpose | Storage |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Instagram handle & public profile data | Enrichment & matching | Google Sheets / Database |
| Name, phone, email | Communication & payouts | Google Sheets / Database |
| Location (area/city) | Geographic matching | Google Sheets / Database |
| Content rates | Deal negotiation | Google Sheets / Database |
| UPI/Bank details | Payment processing | Encrypted storage |
| Conversation logs (IG DM) | Service delivery, quality audit | Agent logs |

**From Brands:**
| Data Type | Purpose | Storage |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Restaurant name & contact info | Outreach & coordination | Google Sheets / Database |
| Cuisine type, area, popular dishes | Campaign matching | Google Sheets / Database |
| WhatsApp conversations | Service delivery | Chatwoot logs |
| Payment history | Invoicing | Google Sheets / Database |

### 2.2 How We Use Data

- **Matching:** Connect the right influencers with relevant brand campaigns
- **Personalization:** AI generates personalized outreach messages using public business information
- **Quality Control:** AI reviews content against campaign briefs
- **Analytics:** Aggregate performance metrics for reporting

### 2.3 AI & Automation Disclosure

- VibMedia uses AI (Google Gemini) for:
  - Personalized first-touch messages (WhatsApp brand outreach)
  - Reply classification and routing
  - Influencer onboarding conversations (via Instagram DM)
  - Content quality review
- **All deal negotiations, pricing discussions, and contract terms are handled by human team members**
- You may be interacting with an AI assistant during initial outreach and onboarding

### 2.4 Data Protection (DPDPA 2023 Compliance)

> **Current Status:** Human-in-the-loop process. Final automated policy will be implemented after legal review.

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of your data at any time by contacting our ops team
- **Right to Correction:** Request correction of any inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data. We will remove non-essential data within 30 days
- **Consent:** By engaging with our outreach and providing your information, you consent to data processing for the stated purposes
- **Opt-out:** Reply "STOP" or "Remove me" to any VibMedia message to opt out. Our team will process the request within 48 hours
- **Data Retention:** Active account data retained while active. Inactive accounts are archived after 12 months. Deleted upon request.

### 2.5 Data Sharing

- We do **NOT** sell personal data to third parties
- Brand-facing campaign reports include influencer handles and content metrics (public data)
- Payment processing data is shared only with payment processors (Razorpay/UPI)
- Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be used for marketing

### 2.6 Security

- All sensitive data (passwords, bank details) is encrypted at rest
- Access to CRM data is restricted to authorized team members
- Instagram session tokens are stored securely with encryption
- Regular access audits conducted

## 3. Influencer Agreement

### 3.1 Joining VibMedia's Creator Network

By completing the onboarding process (via Instagram DM or form), the Influencer agrees to:

1. **Provide accurate information** about their profile, rates, and availability
2. **Complete accepted campaigns** within the agreed timeline
3. **Follow campaign briefs** including deliverables, hashtags, mentions, and posting timelines
4. **Allow AI-assisted onboarding and matching** as part of the service
5. **Accept the commission model** (20% on paid deals)

### 3.2 Influencer Obligations

- Maintain an active, public Instagram account
- Respond to campaign-related messages within 24 hours
- Provide at least 24-hour notice if unable to attend a scheduled visit
- Create original content only (no stock footage, no AI-generated food images)
- Disclose the partnership in compliance with ASCI guidelines ("Paid partnership with [brand]")

### 3.3 What VibMedia Provides

- Free registration and job matching
- AI-powered brand matching based on niche, location, tier, and availability
- Campaign coordination and scheduling
- Content QC review before posting
- Payment processing and commission tracking
- Transparent points and tier progression

### 3.4 Intellectual Property

- Content created by the influencer remains the **influencer's intellectual property**
- Brand receives a **limited license** to repost/share the content on their own channels for 90 days
- VibMedia may use anonymized campaign metrics for marketing purposes

## 4. Brand Service Agreement

### 4.1 Campaign Types

| Type | Description | Typical Budget |
|------|-------------|---------------|
| **Quick Review** | 1 story review of the restaurant experience | ₹0-₹3,000 (barter possible) |
| **Standard Collab** | 1 reel + 2-3 stories | ₹5,000-₹15,000 |
| **Full Campaign** | Multi-content: reels + stories + posts | ₹15,000-₹50,000 |
| **Long-term Partnership** | Monthly retainer with dedicated influencer(s) | ₹25,000-₹1,00,000/mo |

### 4.2 What the Brand Gets

- AI-matched influencer selection based on niche, location, engagement, and tier
- Campaign coordination from scheduling to posting
- AI content quality review before publishing
- Post-campaign performance report (reach, engagement, estimated value)
- Commission-only model — no upfront fees for barter deals

### 4.3 Brand Obligations

- Provide accurate restaurant information (menu, popular dishes, location)
- Ensure the influencer has a positive experience during the visit
- Pay invoices within 7 days of campaign completion
- Provide feedback on influencer content within 48 hours of draft submission
- Not contact influencers directly to bypass VibMedia (circumvention clause)

### 4.4 Pricing & Payment

- Campaign pricing is based on the selected tier (Starter / Growth / Premium)
- **Payment methods:** UPI, bank transfer, or Razorpay link
- **Refund policy:** Full refund if campaign is not started. Pro-rated refund if partially completed. No refund after content is posted.

### 4.5 Cancellation

- Brand may cancel a campaign with 48-hour notice before the scheduled visit
- Late cancellation (within 48 hours) incurs a 25% cancellation fee
- Influencer no-shows result in a free rescheduling at no additional cost

## 5. Acceptable Use Policy

### 5.1 Prohibited Activities (Influencers)

- Purchasing fake followers, likes, or engagement
- Using bots to interact with brand posts
- Accepting campaigns outside VibMedia while using VibMedia-matched brands
- Sharing brand contact information with third parties
- Creating misleading or false content about the brand experience

### 5.2 Prohibited Activities (Brands)

- Contacting influencers directly to bypass VibMedia's commission
- Requesting unpaid work beyond the agreed campaign scope
- Providing false information about the restaurant experience
- Requesting content that violates advertising standards (ASCI guidelines)
- Discriminating against influencers based on protected characteristics

### 5.3 Dispute Resolution

1. **First Contact:** Raise the issue with VibMedia ops team via WhatsApp/Chatwoot
2. **Mediation:** VibMedia mediates between both parties within 72 hours
3. **Resolution:** VibMedia's decision is binding for disputes under ₹25,000
4. **Escalation:** Disputes above ₹25,000 are subject to arbitration in Delhi NCR
