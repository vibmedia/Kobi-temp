# Brand Outreach Templates — WhatsApp

## Day 1: Custom Hook
```
Hi {restaurant_name}! 👋
We noticed your {popular_dish} looks amazing 🔥
We connect restaurants like yours with food creators who make viral reels.
Interested in getting your dishes featured? Takes 2 minutes to discuss.
```

## Day 2: Social Proof Follow-up
```
Hey {contact_name}, just circling back!
{nearby_restaurant} in {area} just got 45K views from a creator campaign with us.
Would love to help {restaurant_name} get similar results 📊
```

## Day 3: Free Deal Hook
```
Last message from us! 🙏
We'd love to offer {restaurant_name} a FREE influencer visit —
a creator comes, eats, shoots a reel. No cost to you.
Just want to show you how it works. Deal?
```

## Post-Deal Confirmation
```
Great news! 🎉
We've matched {influencer_name} ({follower_count} followers)
for your campaign.
They'll visit on {visit_date}.
We'll share the draft video for your approval before posting.
```

# Influencer Outreach Templates — Instagram DMs

## Day 1: Intro
```
Hey {name}! 👋
I'm from VibMedia — we connect Delhi food creators with restaurants for paid collabs.
Loved your recent post about {recent_post_topic}!
Would you be interested in getting regular brand deals through us?
```

## Day 2: Follow-up
```
Hi {name}, just checking in!
We have a few restaurant campaigns coming up in {area}.
Free meals + paid collabs available. Interested?
```

## Day 3: Value Prop
```
Hey {name}! One more nudge 😊
127 food creators in Delhi already get deals through us.
Takes 2 mins to join — just reply here and I'll get you set up!
```

## Onboarding Start (After Positive Reply)
```
Awesome! Welcome to VibMedia's creator network! 🚀
Quick questions to match you with the right brands:
1. What area are you based in?
```
